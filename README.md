## About me

I like building ML systems that do not just work in notebooks, but become part of real products.

I am an ML Engineer with experience in production-grade AI systems, recommender systems, search ranking and data-driven products. Currently, I work at Ozon in the recommendations team, focusing on ranking selected candidates and improving recommendation quality at the ranking stage.

Previously, I worked at Sber on search personalization and ranking systems, contributing to base search, learning-to-rank and click-based ranking models using OpenSearch, LTR, C++, ML models and A/B testing.

I also have experience designing AI assistants based on NLP and RAG architectures, building agent-based systems and developing internal analytics tools that connect CRM data, calls and documents.

My main interests are recommender systems, neural ranking, information retrieval, deep learning and scalable ML systems.
